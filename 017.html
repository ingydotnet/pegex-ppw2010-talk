Pegex Contact Grammar
     
     contact:
         /Name<COLON><WS>+/ <name> <BREAK>
         /Address<COLON><BREAK>/ <address>
         [ /Phone<COLON><WS>+/ <phone> <BREAK> ]?
     name: <first_name> <WS>+ <last_name>
     address: <street> <BREAK> <city> <COMMA>
         <WS>+ <state> <WS>+ <zipcode>
     first_name: /<WORD>+/
     last_name: /<WORD>+/
     street: /<ANY>+/
     state: /<UPPER><UPPER>/
     zipcode: /<DIGIT>{5}/
     phone: /<DIGIT>{3}<DASH><DIGIT>{3}<DASH><DIGIT>{4}/
